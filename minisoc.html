MiniSOC

the compition that we participated in focused on the next subject where we can use AI to enhance the cybersecurity

The MiniSOC is a modern, scalable platform based on SOC designed to adapt to the needs of organizations of all sizes. MiniSOC provides a practical and cost-effective solution to detect, prevent, and respond to cybersecurity threats. Built with simplicity and flexibility in mind, it offers a streamlined approach to cybersecurity without sacrificing the ability to grow and adapt as your organization evolves.

its features are :
1-Continuously monitors for security threats such as malware, phishing, and unauthorized access. Uses tools like intrusion detection systems (IDS) and log monitoring to identify suspicious activitie.
2-Handles common threats automatically, such as blocking malicious IPs or isolating compromised devices. Ensures quick and efficient action with minimal manual input.
3-Utilizes affordable, open-source solutions like Snort for monitoring and analysis. Integrates basic SIEM systems for centralized threat management.
4-Requires only 1-2 cybersecurity analysts or IT staff to manage operations. Designed to function efficiently with limited resources. -Easy to implement without extensive hardware or infrastructure which is ideal for organizations with constrained budgets.
5-Provides clear alerts and predefined steps for responding to security incidents. Includes simple tools for monitoring and tracking threats over time.
